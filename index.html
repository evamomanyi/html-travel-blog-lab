The Scenary

Fire Island has a breath-taking scenary. Surrounded by the blue waters of the Indian Ocean, the island greets you with refreshing air. The Warui people that live in the Island are friendly and the hotel accomodation was top notch. There is plenty of seafood, exotic fruits, plantains and refreshing rum-based cocktails.
